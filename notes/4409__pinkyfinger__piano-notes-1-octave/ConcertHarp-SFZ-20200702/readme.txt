Concert Harp
------------

Version 2020-07-02

Concert Harp sound bank made with samples from the Versilian Community Sample
Library, a CC0 sample library created by Versilian Studios LLC. Thanks to Sam
Gossner, founder of Versilian Studios, for providing those samples under a
free license and answering our emails.

Performer: K. Smith. Location: Boston, MA. Small room. Microphones: 2x Rode
NT1-A spaced pair, 2x AT Pro 37's close. Interface: Focusrite Scarlett 18i8
(first gen). Recorded on 2015-01-26.

This sound bank is assembled by roberto@zenvoid.org. Samples have been edited,
if you need the original unprocessed sounds, please visit the VCSL repository:
https://vis.versilstudios.com/vcsl.html

This sound bank, in several formats and qualities, is available on the
FreePats project web pages: http://freepats.zenvoid.org/
